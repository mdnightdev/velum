Introduction

Velum ("we," "us," "our") is dedicated to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how it is utilized, and the measures we employ to secure it when you access our Platform.

By using the Platform, you acknowledge that you have read and understood the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our data management practices, please terminate your account immediately.

Security Model: Our storage utilizes AES-256-GCM symmetric encryption for high-risk attributes at rest, and credential verification utilizes Argon2id password-hashing with unique salts. No plaintext passwords or decryption keys are ever sent to or processed by the server database.

1. Information Collection

1.1 User-Provided Information

In operating your account, we handle:

  • Cryptographic Digests: Zero-knowledge hashes of your password, Safe Word, lockdown phrase, and recovery key. We do not store plaintext credentials.
  • Profile Attributes: Optional avatar selections, display name, public biography, and derived location.
  • Communications: Message contents, files, and ticket support conversations. Private communications are encrypted and isolated within active sessions.
  • Transactions: Escrow transaction records, marketplace item descriptions, and user feedback ratings.

1.2 Automatically Collected System Metadata

  • Hardware Information: Browser type, screen geometry, client user agent, and device fingerprints used for fraud prevention.
  • IP Address Logs: Captured to enforce sliding-window rate limits, detect session hijacking, and block unauthorized access.
  • Telemetry: Session duration, login attempts, activity timestamps, and message volumes.

3. Security Architecture & Retention

3.1 Encryption & Protection

Data at rest is secured via AES-256-GCM. Sessions are authenticated using cryptographic challenges containing one-time nonces to prevent credential replay attacks. Brute-force attacks are thwarted using exponential rate-limiting backoffs.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell user data. Information is disclosed only under the following strictly defined scenarios:

  • Legal Enforcement: Where legally mandated by Taiwanese regulatory authorities or courts.
  • Escrow Resolution: To Platform mediators in the event of a transaction dispute.
  • Technical Service Providers: Encrypted payloads transferred to database storage and cloud infrastructure providers under strict privacy agreements.
